Veg Biryani

Biryani is a mixed rice dish, mainly popular in South Asia. It is made with rice, some type of meat and spices. To cater to vegetarians, in some cases, it is prepared by substituting vegetables or paneer for the meat.

Ingrediens

  • 1 cup boiled basmati rice
  • 1/2 teaspoon mint leaves
  • salt as required

Procedure

  1. Prepare saffron-kewra water and chop veggies
  2. Saute the onions
  3. Cook biryani on low heat for 5-6 minutes
  4. Serve hot chicken biryani with your favourite chutney or raita
